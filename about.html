About Fran

Holly Fran is the proud owner and creative heart behind Fran's Flowers. Her love for floral design began in childhood, watching her mother, Sarah Fran, bring beauty and meaning into every arrangement at the shop she opened in 1990. Surrounded by blooms and the stories of the people who walked through the door, Holly grew up with a deep appreciation for how flowers can express what words sometimes cannot.

Over the years, Holly's passion for floral artistry grew into her own craft. She learned not just how to arrange flowers, but how to listenâ€”to the occasion, the sentiment, and the person behind each request. When the time came to carry on her mother's legacy, Holly embraced the opportunity with joy, respect, and a fresh creative spirit.

Today, Holly continues to lead Fran's Flowers with thoughtful design, intentional craftsmanship, and a warm, personal approach to every order. Whether it's a celebration, a comfort, or an everyday moment of beauty, Holly believes every bouquet should feel as unique as the people it represents.
